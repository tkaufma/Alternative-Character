BOOK REVIEWS

"The Crimson Witch, a paranormal novel for all who enjoy a spooky treat, finds the young girl of Daniella Brochuette in an exciting adventure through the discovery of her family's secrets. Brochuette is an riveting protagonist, a high school student who is thrown into the chaotic world of her father's secret other life." --Angela Poertis, The County Review "The Girl With The Crescent Moon Tattoo, a great page-turner for those who love thrillers, takes on the journey of the young girl of Francesca Mial through the rollercoaster which is the life of Kai Jaisen. Francesca is warped into the fantasy world of dragons and faes. Mial is a strong-willed character with a loud mouth and feisty heart." --John Kaensten, The Los Angeles Times Review "The Vampire Test, a treat for lovers of vampire romance and mystery, witnesses Missy Hoelin as she discovers the exciting and threatening realm of the world of vampires. Hoelin is an headstrong-protagonist who is in high school and stumbles upon a mysterious who she is forced to speak as a bet with some of her 'so-called' friends." --Kirsty Jenkins, The New York Times
